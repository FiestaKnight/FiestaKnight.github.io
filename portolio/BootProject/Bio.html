Hi I'm William Frank! A normal guy with a love for walking anywhere and everywhere I can!


For as long as I can remember I could walk. Even my family said I never crawled, but got on my feet and started moving my legs. For me it's always been one of my favorite stress relief activities and with a group it's how I've been able to catch up with someone or break the ice.

With that said I have some walking facts for you!

Walking Facts!
