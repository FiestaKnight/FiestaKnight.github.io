Why I Like It

A trip down memory lane!

The original Rivals of Aether was one of the games I played a lot to connect with a dear friend of mine over in Sweden which makes it hold a special place in my heart.

I would mostly play Etalus and guest character Shovel Knight, who both are not in the current game roster, while my other friend would play basically the rest of the cast.

We mostly stuck to playing with each other opposed to with strangers and that time spent was always a blast!
